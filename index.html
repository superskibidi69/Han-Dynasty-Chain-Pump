Chain pumps in the Han Dynasty

What is the chain pump?

Chain pumps is totally my new invention. It consists of square boards rotating on a circlular chain.

Why is this important, and how does it work?

Farmers used to have to pull water with buckets before this innovation. With this tool, they only need manpower for pulling a pedal, which rotates everything on the chain, and the square boards trap water, which is emptied into an irrigation ditch.

This is what the chain pump looks like. Animals or people can drive pedals to rotate the chain.
